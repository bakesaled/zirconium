Zirconium

Zirconium is a traffic-flow puzzle game. Gain points for each car that passes through the intersection. Tap or click the intersection to trigger a traffic signal cycle and keep traffic flowing. Traffic density increases as the game play levels advance. Don't let traffic back up too far, otherwise the game ends.

Object of the Game

To allow as many cars through the intersection as possible.
