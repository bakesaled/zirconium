Zirconium

Description

Zirconium is a traffic-flow puzzle game. Gain points for each car that passes through the intersection. Tap or click the intersection to change the traffic lights and keep traffic flowing. Traffic density increases as the gameplay levels advance. Don't let traffic back up too far, otherwise, the game ends.

Object of the Game

To allow as many cars through the intersection as possible.
